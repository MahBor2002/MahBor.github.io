Quick introduction about me

My name is Mahdad Borhani. i try to learn new things on my own and try to teach them to others. I have a strong interest in astronomy, spaceflight, physics, programming, robotics, electronics and mechanics. My other interests include chemistry, biology, engineering, philosophy and other general science. I love looking at something and knowing the exact mechanics of how it works. I love finding answers to both big important and also silly questions. questions like why the sky is blue, why plants are green, why living organisms don't have wheels, how gravitation works(it's not how people think it is), how to tile pavements better, how drivers navigate, why smoke is white, how thinking works, how trees lure animals and many more questions. These questions make me see the world in a much more beautiful way. I've been told i see the world slightly differently than how others see it.


I currently work on some programming, learning and teaching. I learn programming, physics, rocket design, chemistry, engineering, robotics, electronics, CAD and modeling.
For the past 3.5 years I've been working on my ATP program. APT stands for Astronaut Training Program and is my biggest and longest on-going project. More about my projects can be found on the projects tab at the top of this webpage.
